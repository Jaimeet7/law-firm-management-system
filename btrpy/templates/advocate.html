About

Pearson Hardman LLP is a prestigious corporate law firm headquartered in Midtown Manhattan at 601 E 54th Street, New York City. Founded in 2003 by managing partners Daniel Hardman and Jessica Pearson, the firm quickly established itself as one of the top-tier legal outfits in the city. Pearson Hardman is recognized for its exclusive recruitment policy—hiring only Harvard Law graduates—and its focus on high-stakes corporate matters, especially in mergers & acquisitions, litigation, and financial regulation. The firm is known for its rigorous work culture, hierarchical structure, and emphasis on loyalty and performance.
